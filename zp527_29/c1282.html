Between the Pagan Past and Christian Present in Byzantine Visual Culture: Statues in Constantinople, 4th-13th Centuries CE

著者名: Paroma Chatterjee 出版者: Cambridge University Press 出版年: 06 Jan 2022 版表示: シリーズ: 2022/03/24 HardbackThis book is about the importance of pre-Christian, pagan statues in Byzantine art which has so far focused heavily on the sacred icons of Christ and the saints. It will interest those seeking an accessible and interesting study on topics such as art, history, religion, literature and ideas related to the Roman Empire.
